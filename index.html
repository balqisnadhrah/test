About

Welcome to HomeLand.

This distributed app runs on the Etherereum blockchain network and holds a tenancy deposit as Solidity smart contract.

  • Tenancy Deposit Solidity Contract:
    • You can start by choosing house, and then leave a message that you want to rent a house (which house, Your Name, Your IC Num, and the duration).
    • Landlord will create contract by filling the deposit tenant need to pay.
    • Tenant then will pay the deposit using Ethereum.
    • When the contract ended, landlord can terminate the contract.
  • Landlord could:
    • create a new tenancy contract
    • Terminate contract
  • Tenant could:
    • sign a tenancy contract thus paying the deposit amount
    • Terminate contract
